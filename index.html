Click Here to see the High Scores

To play, press the "Start" button below and your timer will start.

You'll be given 10 questions with four choices for you to answer. Pick the best choice and move on. Pick incorrectly and lose 15 seconds from the timer.

When all the questions are done or you run out of time, the game ends and you'll get your score, which is points for correct answers plus a bonus for time. Top 10 scores get your name saved for eternity!
